Kung Fu Panda

Po's name means "precious" in Mandarin Chinese, reflecting his endearing personality.

Po has a strong appetite for food, particularly dumplings, a recurring theme in the series.

Po is mentored by Master Shifu, who helps him fulfill his destiny as the Dragon Warrior.

The voice of Po in the Kung Fu Panda films is provided by the famous actor Jack Black.
